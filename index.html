INTRODUCTION

My name is Rajat Ahuja. I'm currently a junior studying Computer Engineering and Plan II Honors at the University of Texas at Austin. My interests lie mostly in the fields of software engineering and IoT, but I also enjoy fields such as web development and embedded systems. I have experience in both low-level and high-level programming languages. Through my coursework, I have gained a strong foundational programming background in ARM Assembly, C, and C++. I'm currently interested in pursuing a software engineering internship this semester in Austin, however I'm also interested in positions in Embedded Systems and IoT. Ideally this internship will take 10-15 hours of my time during the week, but I'm willing to take on more responsibility depending on the role.
